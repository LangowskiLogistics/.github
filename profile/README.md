## 🏢 About

**Langowski Logistics** is an international logistics company delivering comprehensive transport and supply chain solutions across Europe and beyond. This GitHub organization is the home of our internal engineering team — the people who build and maintain the digital backbone of our operations.

We develop software that keeps logistics moving: from shipment tracking and process automation, to data pipelines and internal tooling. Everything here is purpose-built, production-grade, and deeply integrated with the way we work.

<br/>

---

## ⚙️ What We Build

| Area | Description |
|---|---|
| 🖥️ **Internal Web Apps** | Custom dashboards, portals, and management tools tailored to logistics workflows |
| 🔄 **Process Automation** | Background workers, scheduled jobs, and event-driven pipelines that eliminate manual work |
| 📦 **Logistics Systems** | Operational software for tracking, planning, and coordinating transport processes |
| 📊 **Data & Reporting** | ETL pipelines, reporting backends, and analytics integrations |
| 🔌 **Integrations** | Connectors between internal systems, third-party platforms, and external APIs |

## 🔒 Access & Security

All repositories in this organization are **private** and intended strictly for internal use.

Access is granted exclusively to authorized team members. If you're a Langowski employee and need access, contact the development team or your team lead directly.

> ⚠️ Unauthorized access attempts are logged and reported.
